Model Worldwide
Terrain Egypt

Kabul

This scenario takes place in North Egypt, with an opposition between a blue force, a local red asymetric force and a neutral population.

Some scores are calculated and allow the player to follow the mean operational state of the red units, the blue units and the combattant blue units.

Profiles

  • Supervisor: scripted actions allow you to demonstrate the scenario and the use of scores
  • Blue: to actually play the scenario

Objectives

  • Destroy the red forces
  • Protect the population
