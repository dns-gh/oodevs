Mission : AAA - Defend area

Context

To defend Army ground forces against attack by enemy aircraft.
In Sword, to move to and occupy an observation position in order to detect and engage flying enemies within the given area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Area

The area to defend against enemies aircrafts.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Position

The position from which the agent observe the area.

ProbabilityToHit

The probability to hit (PH) used by the agent to engage enemies entering into the area (a low PH allows the agent to engage far enemies with less attrition, a high PH allows the agent to engage nearby enemies with high attrition).

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward the given position. If it is not reachable (an urban block inside which the agent cannot move, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible; otherwise it moves to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities regarding the terrain properties.
  • If not issued, the agent stays at its own position.
  • Upon reaching the position, the agent starts to observe and wait for enemies to come.
  • While observing the area, the agent engage all flying enemies that enter the area.

Specific cases
  • During the mission, if the position parameter has not be issued, user may change the observation position of the agent by issued the 'GoTo' fragmentary order.

Comments

* The agents engage only flying enemies,
  • The agents won't open fires if:
    • known enemies are out of range regarding the weapon systems and remaining dotations,
    • known enemies are not in sight (a enemies may be known but not perceived) regarding sensors and terrain.
  • known enemies are not flying
  • Ensure that the given position is chosen with care, regarding the perception capability of the agent and the given objective.

End of mission
