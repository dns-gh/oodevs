Mission : TRANS - Deploy (on a position)

Context

Set-up adopted by a unit for immediate ore later action.
In SWORD, for agents, to know how to move toward a deployment position and simply self deploy onto it.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Deployment position

Position to reach to deploy onto it (empty by default).

Behavior
  • If the Deployment position is not issued the agent deploys on its own position.
  • If a position is issued, the agent moves toward it and self deploy onto itthe report "starting to deploy" then section deployed" should be displayed). The deployment may take delays (see "deployment" section in "units" tab).

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmantary order) is issued by user.
