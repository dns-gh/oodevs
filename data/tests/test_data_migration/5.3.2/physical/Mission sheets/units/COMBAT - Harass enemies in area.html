Mission : COMBAT - Harass enemies in area

Context

Mission to destroy or at least to neutralize the enemy hidden elements, infiltrated or during infiltration and exfiltration in a given area.
In SWORD, to observe an area and to occupy positions in order to engage identified units in this area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Area

The area into which the agent will shoot the enemies.

Meeting position

Meeting position to reach upon mission completion.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Positions

Positions the agent will occupy to shoot the enemies.

Permanent

Whether or not the agent will stop the mission when all the positions have been occupied.

Behavior

The agent reaches a position (selected automatically by the agent among the given positions) to observe the area. The agent will therefore observe this objective so that it will engage the enemies: any identified enemy that passes inside the area will, if possible, be destroyed. Every time the agent fires, it will move to a previously unoccupied position, until all the positions have been occupied. The agent then starts over from the first position, or ends his mission by going to the meeting position, depending on the value of the boolean "Permanent" (by default, the agent will go the meeting position).
