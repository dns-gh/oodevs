Mission : SAFETY - Lift confinement on urban blocks

Context

In the event of an exterior threat (a fire, for instance), populations may be prevented from leaving the buildings they occupy.
In SWORD, lifting confinement on an urban block allows the population to leave or enter it. This mission is the reverse of the "Confiine urban blocks within area" mission. Its goal is to lift confinement of a set of urban blocks within a specified zone.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Urban blocks

An ordered list of populated areas in which a population can be confined.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior

The agent moves toward each provided urban block. The agent stops at the border of the urban block.
Upon reaching a urban block, the agent lifts confinement on it before moving to the next unvisited urban block.
When all the urban blocks have been visited, the unit goes to the rally point.

Specific cases

The confinement may not be lifted on the urban block if it cannot be populated (the report "no population in the urban block" is displayed). In that specific case, the agent decides to act on the next block.

Comments

End of mission

When the rally point is reached, the mission ends.
