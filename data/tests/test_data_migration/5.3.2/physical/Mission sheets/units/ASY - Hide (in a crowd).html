Mission : ASY - Hide (in a crowd)

Context

To hide in a given crowd in order to perform infiltration into a safety device.
In Sword, for asymmetric threats (such as terrorists) to follow the head of a crowd trying to stay within it (the agent goes undetected).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Crowd

Crowd to hide into.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent goes undetected (magic effect) and then chooses and moves to a position in order to stay as close as possible to the front of the given crowd. If the position is not reachable (an urban block inside which the agent cannot move, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible, else it moves to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities regarding the terrain properties.
  • The chosen position changes dynamically if the followed crowd moves.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmentary order) is issued by user.
