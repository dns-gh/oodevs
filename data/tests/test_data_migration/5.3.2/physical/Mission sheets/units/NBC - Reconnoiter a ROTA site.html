Mission : NBC - Reconnoiter a ROTA site

Context

A mission undertaken to obtain information by visual observation, or other detection methods, about the activities and resources of an enemy, or about the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area.
For NBC reconnaisssance units, to predict and warn of hazard areas from NBC attacks and from ROTA (release other than attack). It allows evaluation of NBC information and the influence of NBC incidents on operations.
In Sword, to reach a ROTA site objective in order to gather specific informations on it (danger level and type of NBC agent).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Site

ROTA site to reconnoiter.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent protects itself with NBC protection and then moves toward the site. If it is not reachable (it is located on a urban block inside which the agent cannot move, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible, otherwise it moves to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities with regards to terrain properties.
  • Upon reaching the site, the agent starts the reconnaissance (the report "reconnaissance started [object name]" should be displayed). After a period of time depending on the size of the objective, the reconnaissance ends (the report "reconnaissance completed" should be displayed), and the mission terminates.
