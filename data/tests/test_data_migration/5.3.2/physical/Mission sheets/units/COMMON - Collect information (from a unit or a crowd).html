Mission : COMMON - Collect information (from a unit or a crowd)

Context

To gather important information about a specific situation; units may interrogate prisoners or collect information from
civilians and local authorities.
In SWORD, to get a specific message information from a unit or a crowd. An interrogated unit or crowd display a message written by trainers, in order to provide indication(s) to trainees about the situation. The interrogated unit can represent a local authority, a journalist etc.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Entity (crowd or unit)

Unit or crowd to get the information from.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent moves to the entity and gets the required information after a defined period of time. For the unit that is being interrogated the report "being interrogated" should be displayed.
  • If the agent tries to interrogate an enemy unit, the force ratio must be high in order to get the information. Else, the report "impossible to collect information: force is low" should be displayed.

Specific cases

Comments

Gathering information from a unit takes time. How long it takes depends on:
  • the affinity of the interrogated unit with regards to the agent's camp: the more the interrogated unit has affinity with the agent, the faster it acquires information (see delays in authoring tool).
  • the diplomacy matrix: an enemy cannot be interrogated if the force ratio of the agent (the interrogator) is low.

End of mission

The mission ends once the unit gets the information.
