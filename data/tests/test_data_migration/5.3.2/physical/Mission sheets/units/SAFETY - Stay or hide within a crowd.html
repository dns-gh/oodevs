Mission : SAFETY - Stay or hide within a crowd

Context

In SWORD, a mission in which one unit stays within a given crowd. This mission may be used by an agent to hide (terrorists), to monitor or to escort a crowd (police).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Crowd

the crowd inside which the agent must stay.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior

The agent will moves to the center of the issued crowd. Once it is reached, it stops. If the crowd is moving, the agent will follow the head of the crowd (this mission doesn't ensure the agent to stay in the crowd while it is moving. Staying in the crowd depends on the computed path).

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission continue until another one is issued.
