Mission : COMMON - Request take off

Context

To take off from a position while being transported by other unit(s)
In Sword, to request the taking off of the units currently transporting the agent. This mission is used to coordinate the aircrafts, when SWORD is connected to other simulations through an HLA federation.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Takeoff position

Takeoff time

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior
  • The transporting units move toward the given position. If it is not reachable (a urban block inside which the units cannot move for instance), the units move as close as possible, otherwise they move to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities regarding the terrain properties.
  • Upon reaching the position, the agent is ready to take off .

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends once the unit has taken off.
