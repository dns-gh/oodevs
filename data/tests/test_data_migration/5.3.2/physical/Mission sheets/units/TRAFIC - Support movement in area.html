Mission : TRAFIC - Support movement in area

Context

All actions performed by the units in traffic control to support the projection by land forces and movements according to the priorities set by the command in an area.
In SWORD, to create a movement enhanced area (an object) where friend agents move at their maximum speed inside it.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Movement enhanced area

(planned object): the movement enhanced area object to perform.

Optional

Phase lines

Limit 1

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward the movement forbidden area to perform. The position reached to perform it is automatically determinated by the agent.
  • Upon reaching a construction position, the agent constructs instantaneously the object. The report "work just started" should be displayed.
  • At the end of the mission, the movement enhanced area is instantaneously removed.

Specific cases

Comments

The speed of any friend agent which moves inside the movement enhanced area will be increased at his maximum.

End of mission

The mission continues until another mission.
