Mission : SAFETY - Follow and reinforce

Context

In SWORD, a mission in which one unit augments the capability of another unit.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Target unit

the agent to reinforce.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior

The agent will moves to the target unit and follow it if the target unit is moving. Once it is reached, it reinforces it (no specific report is displayed here).

Specific cases

The target unit may not be reinforced if cannot be reached (the agent won't follow the target unit). In that case, the report "Cannot reach objective" will be displayed and the mission ends.

Comments

End of mission

The mission continues until another one is issued.
