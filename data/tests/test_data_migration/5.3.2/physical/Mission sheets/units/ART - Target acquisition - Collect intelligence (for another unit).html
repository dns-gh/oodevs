Mission : ART - Target acquisition - Collect intelligence (for another unit)

Context

The systematic observation of aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic, or other means. To provide continuous view, and the potential for reports on the activity of an objective
In this Sword mission, to follow a unit (a company or platoon, for instance) and get information about the presence of units within the given area (by applying a magic effect).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected ennemy direction.

Area

Area into which the agent detects units.

Unit

The entity to support (a platoon or a company).

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Distance

The distance between the follower (the agent) and the followed (the supported company or unit) ( in meters, default value is 100 meters).

Behavior

The agent follows the given unit, staying at a defined distance (100 meters by default) while detecting units in the given area.

Specific cases

An entity may not be followed if it is not operational (for units). In this case, the report "The followed unit is destroyed" is displayed. If none of the issued agents can be followed, the mission ends with a 'failed' status, displaying the report "No valid parameter". If this is the case the mission terminates.

Comments

If a company (automaton) is issued, the agent follows the command post, at the defined distance.

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmentary order) is issued by user.
