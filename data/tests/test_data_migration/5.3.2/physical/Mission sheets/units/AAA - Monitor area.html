Mission : AAA - Monitor area

Context

The systematic observation of aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic, or other means. To provide continuous view, and the potential for reports on the activity of an objective.
In Sword, to move to and occupy an observation position in order to detect flying enemies within the given area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Area

Objective(s) to defend.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Position

Position from which the agent monitors the area and fires at enemies.

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward the given position. If it is not reachable (an urban block inside which the agent cannot move, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible; otherwise it moves to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities regarding the terrain properties.
  • If not issued, the agent stays at its own position.
  • Upon reaching the position, the agent starts to observe and wait for enemies to come.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmentary order) is issued by user.
