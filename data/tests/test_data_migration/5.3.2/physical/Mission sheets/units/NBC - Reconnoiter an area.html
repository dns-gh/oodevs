Mission : NBC - Reconnoiter an area

Context

A mission undertaken to obtain information by visual observation, or other detection methods, about the activities and resources of an enemy, or about the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area.
For NBC reconnaisssance units, to predict and warn of hazard areas from NBC attacks and from ROTA (release other than attack). It allows evaluation of NBC information and the influence of NBC incidents on operations.
In Sword, to reach an area in order to detect specific objects such as "contaminated area", unknown "ROTA site" etc.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Area

Area to reconnoiter.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent self-protects with NBC protection and then moves toward the center of the area. If it is not reachable (the center is located in an urban block the agent cannot enter, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible, otherwise it moves to the center of the position. Average speed depends on the physical capabilities with regard to terrain properties.
  • Upon reaching the center of the area, the agent starts the reconnaissance (the report "reconnaissance started" should be displayed). After a period of time depending on the size of the area, the reconnaissance ends (the report "reconnaissance completed" should be displayed), and the mission terminates.
