Mission : NBC - Decontaminate an area

Context

To depollute or decontaminate in order to rehabilitate a site or a soil that has been polluted by industrial activities. The decontamination of a site reduces the risk of pollution to acceptable levels or make it disappear.
In SWORD, decontaminating a site ("contaminated site") by NBC unit prevents units from being contaminated. It prevents the creation and the dispersion of a contaminated cloud that can cause injuries in units. A "decontaminated site" in SWORD is defined by a surface delimited by a polygon. All contamination objects (such as "contaminated area") that intersect with the decontaminated surface lose their contamination capacity.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Area

Area to decontaminate.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior

The agent put on his CBRN protection (the report "CBRN suit put on" should be displayed). The agent moves toward the area.if possible, the agent moves to the center, otherwise ("impossible to go through this" should be displayed) it moves to a close position (the position closest to the center).
The agent begins to decontaminate it, the report "starting to decontaminate" should be displayed. When the agent finish to decontaminate, the report "decontamination done" should be displayed.

Specific cases

Comments

The time to decontaminate an area is depening of the time to agent to be posted.

End of mission

 At the end of the decontamination, the agent stays at its own position and the mission ends.
