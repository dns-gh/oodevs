Mission : ENGINEER - Remove mines from obstacles

Context

"The technique of clearing mines using either mechanical sweeping to remove, disturb, or otherwise neutralize the mine; explosive sweeping to cause sympathetic detonations, damage, or displace the mine; or influence sweeping to produce either the acoustic or magnetic influence required to detonate the mine."
In sword, removing mines improvement is to make the "mining" attribute of object goes from 100% to 0%. By loosing mines improvement, an obstacle looses its attrition power in case a unit collides with it.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Obstacles

The obstacles to remove the mines from.

Meeting position

Position to reach upon action termination.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Behavior

* The agent moves towards each obstacle, and remove mines imprevement from it.
* When the agent has swept all the obstacles, it moves to the meeting position.

Specific cases

If some of the obstacles were already cleared, they will not be considered (the report "The object is already demined" should be displayed).

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all the obstacles have been cleared and the agent has reached the meeting position.
