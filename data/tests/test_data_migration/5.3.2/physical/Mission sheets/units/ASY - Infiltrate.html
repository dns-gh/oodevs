Mission : ASY - Infiltrate

Context

A process in which individuals (or small groups) penetrate an area (especially the military penetration of enemy positions without detection)
In sword, for "undetectable" units, to move along a route (designated by waypoints) in order to infiltrate a device.
(Asymetric units such as terrorists, individuals such as journalist go undetected in sword, to simulate their ability to be unnoticed)

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Waypoints

The waypoints to move along.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward each waypoint defining the route. If a visited waypoint is not reachable (an urban block inside which the agent cannot move, for instance), the agent moves as close as possible, otherwise it moves to the center of the position.
  • Average speed depends on the physical capabilities taking into account terrain properties.
  • Upon reaching a waypoint, the agent moves to the next one and so on, until the final waypoint is reached.

Specific cases
  • If the computed routes computed between two waypoints go through a checkpoint, the agent may be detected and captured.

Comments

End of mission

When there are no more waypoints to visit, the agent stays next to the last one and the mission ends.
