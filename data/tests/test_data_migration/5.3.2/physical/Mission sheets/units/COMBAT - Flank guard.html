Mission : COMBAT - Flank guard

Context

"The action of a force which aids, protects, complements, or sustains another force in accordance with a directive requiring such action."
In this Sword mission, to move toward the provided positions and shoot at all enemies in range with direct fires.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Positions

Positions the agent will occupy to flank guard friends.

Friends

Friends unit to flank guard

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent performs the mission.

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward the given positions. When there are several positions, the agent choses the closest to the friends units.
  • The agent will engage all known and perceived enemies in range.
* Initial position

* Agent moves to first position

* Agent is reaching the closest position between him and the friend to flank guard

Specific cases

To make the agent engage enemies upon order, the user may set the "ROE (Rules Of Engagement) on "fire upon order".

Comments
  • The agents won't open fires if:
    • known enemies are out of range regarding the weapon systems and remaining dotations,
    • known enemies are not in sight (a enemies may be known but not perceived) regarding sensors and terrain.
  • Ensure that the given position is chosen with care, regarding the perception capability of the agent and the given objective.

End of mission

This mission continues until another one is issued.
