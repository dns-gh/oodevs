Mission : Crowd - Move to

Context

To move to an objective.
In SWORD, a moving crowd is modeled as a flux with a constant flow, which can be altered by terrain and obstacles.

Parameters

Mandatory

WayPoints

Objective to reach.

Optional

Behavior
  • The agent moves toward the objective, following a computed route. If an obstacle is encountered on the way to the objective, the crowd stops and a new temporary concentration is created. The mission is suspended until the obstacle is removed.
  • The agent is considered as entirely moved when the tail of the flux has reached the given objective.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

Upon movement termination, the mission terminates.
