Mission : ENGINEER - Remove obstacles within area

Context

To reduce or negate the effects of existing or reinforcing obstacles. The objectives are to maintain freedom of movement for maneuver units, weapons systems and critical supplies.
In SWORD, to know how to remove a set of given obstacles (such as mines, barricades etc.).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

Area into which the subordinates must remove retreived obstacles.

Optional

Phase lines

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Remove obstacles within area" mission to the engineer platoons, with a sub-area as objective. Each subordinate perform its mission by reconnoitering its sub-area and then by removing objects considered as 'obstacles'.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission terminates when all subordinates has finished their mission.
