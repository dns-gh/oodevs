Mission : OOTW - Control a crowd

Context

To prevent the outbreak of disorder and prevention of possible riot.
In Sword, to move to a crowd and try to control it by activating a control action. The action to control a crowd can take delays and can require help from others units.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Crowd

The crowd to control.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Control a crowd" mission to the subordinate units.
  • Units that cannot perform the mission support or follow the front units (units that will perform the mission).

  • The mission may fail if the subordinate units fail to control the given crowd:
