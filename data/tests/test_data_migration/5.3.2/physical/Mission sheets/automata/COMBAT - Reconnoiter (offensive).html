Mission : COMBAT - Reconnoiter (offensive)

Context

A mission undertaken to obtain information by visual observation, or other detection methods, about the activities and resources of an enemy, or about the meteorological, hydrographic, or geographic characteristics of a particular area.
In Sword to reach a set of ordered terrain objectives in order to search for enemies, or specific objects (such as mines and various obstacles) by activating a specific "reconnaissance" action. Agents of the simulation are able to reconnoiter objects, specific location and urban blocks. The delay of reconnaissance depends on the type of the objective to reconnoiter. The bigger the objective, the longer the reconnaissance is.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Objectives

Objectives to reconnoiter.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Search objectives

Echelon number

Define if the maneuver is organized into one or two echelons (default value is '2').

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Reconnoiter (offensive)" mission to subordinate front line units (the number of subordinate to perform the mission depends on the number of front line units and the number of subordinates able to perform the 'Reconnoiter (offensive)' mission).
  • Forward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Reconnoiter" mission to each of the forward echelon units,
    • The agent allocates an sub-area of responsability to each of the foward echelon units. Each subordinate unit reconnoiter objectives inside the its area of responsability (sub objectives can be computed by the company to keep te units aligned).
  • Rear echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "mobility support" mission to the engineer units, with the front units as parameters.
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the front units as parameter.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear line may receive the "combat support" mission (the enemy may be engaged),
    • In case of a front line unit destruction, combat units of the rear line may releive this unit.
    • Upon issuing the mission, the commander agent coordinates its subordinate units. The agent keeps the forward echelon units aligned.
    • Upon reaching a coordination line or the departure line (if issued), the commander agent waits the "continue maneuver" frago to go on. In that cas, the report "waiting for frag to cross the line of departure" is displayed.

Specific cases

Note that the forward echelon units stay aligned with the furthest issued objective.

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all issued objectives has been reconnoitered.
