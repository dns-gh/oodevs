Mission : COMMON - Cross

Context

The purpose of any river crossing is to project combat power across a water obstacle to accomplish a mission. The challenge is to minimize the river's impact on the commander's ability to maneuver.
In Sword, to use given prepared crossing sites object to cross a water obstacle.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Bridge

Bridge object to use to cross the water obstacle.

Destination

Final position to reach.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Cross" mission to all its subordinate units, with the 'bridge' and the 'destination' as parameters.
  • Upon reaching the crossing site, the subordinate units move to the given destination and the mission terminates.
  • The mission may fail if the bridge object is not trafficable. If the bridge is built by engineer units onto a crossing site object, beware of issuing the bridge object, not the crossing site object ( crossing site may not be trafficable).
