Mission : COMBAT - Delay

Context

To delay enemy advance from successive positions
In Sword, to occupy successively a set of destroying positions in order to engage encountered enemies.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Meeting position

Position to reach by the command post and engineer units after performing their mission.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Obstacles

Set of obstacles ot errect by engineer units (no obstacle by default).

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Delay" mission to the teeth arm platoons, with the blocking positions as parameters,
    • The scouting platoons receive the "Disengage" mission with the meeting position as parameter,
    • The engineer units receive the "Perform construction works" mission if obstacles have to be errected (otherwise they receive the "follow" mission)
  • Rear echelon units:
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "Disengage" mission with blocking positions as parameters.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear echelon may receive the "combat support" mission(the enemy may be engaged).
    • In case of a first echelon unit destruction, combat units of the rear echelon may releive this unit.
  • Subordinate units performing the "Delay" mission disengage:
    • if the user issue an explicit order ("Disengage")
    • if the enemy has been engaged and if the force ratio is low
  • All the agent share the same issued area of responsability but receive different blocking positions.
  • Whenever a unit is tactically destroyed, it receives the "Disengage" mission with the meeting position as parameter

Specific cases

Comments

In order for the mission to go smoothly, it is strongly recommended to provide a Start Mission Line (SML) and an END Mission Line (EML).

End of mission

The mission ends when all the subordinate agents have finished their mission.
