Mission : COMBAT - Escort

Context

A member of the Armed Forces assigned to accompany, assist, or guide an individual or group. An armed guard that accompanies a convoy, a train, prisoners, etc.
In SWORD, to accompany a unit toward a specific location in order to protect it against potenital enemies or other dangers. The agent transports the unit to bring it to the given destination. No round trips here. The transportation is done by a magic action. The agent does not need specific physical capabilities.
This mission is offensive. While escorting the unit, the agent that performs the mission will engage all perceived enemies in range.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Unit

The unit to escort to the final objective.

Route

The route followed by the agent.The last waypoint of the route defines the final objective onto which unit must be brought.

Meeting position

The position to be reached by the escorting agent once the escorted units have been successfully brought to the final objective.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • In this mission, only one unit will be the forward echelon: the commander agent issues the "Escort" mission to one units with the 'route' as parameter.
  • Rear echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "combat support" mission to the engineer units, with the front unit as parameters.
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the front units as parameter.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when the escorting units has reached the meeting position.
