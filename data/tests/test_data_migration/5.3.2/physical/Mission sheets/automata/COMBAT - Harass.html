Mission : COMBAT - Harass

Context

Mission to destroy or at least to neutralize the enemy hidden elements, infiltrated or during infiltration and exfiltration in a given area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Objective

Objective where enemies will be engaged if they are inside

Optional

Phase lines

Echelon number

Destroying positions

Positions for observing objective where begin to harass enemies.

Obstacles

Set of obstacles to build. Optional parameter.

Behavior

The commander agent issues the "Harass" mission to the platoons, with objective as objective.
Rear echelon units: other units (non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter. Some others units (like mortars) receive one of the support missions: "suppor indirect fire", "support direct fire" or "support movement" depending of their abilities.

Specific cases
  • If "obstacles" are given as parameter, the subordinates with "harass" mission will be begining by :
    • the subordinates will build obstacles if they have capacity and dotation.
    • if "valorise obstacles" is true, the subordinates will valorise the obstacles if they have capacity and dotation.
  • "Destroying positions" for subordinates with "harass" mission:
    • if given, the subordinates will move between two of these positions and uses it like an observating position.
    • otherwise, two positions inside the objective will be given as parameter for each subordinate

Comments

End of mission

The mission continues until another one is issued.
