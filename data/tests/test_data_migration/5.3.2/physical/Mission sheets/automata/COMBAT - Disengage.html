Mission : COMBAT - Disengage

Context

To disengage from enemies, breaking contact with the enemi and moving to a point where the enemy can neither observe nor engage the unit by direct fire.
In SWORD, disengaging from enemies or a threat is to move toward a given position, with a very high priority. In this mission the agents of the company try to reach the issued position at all cost. The position is chosen and issued by the user.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Position

Position to reach.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • Forward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Disengage" mission to each of the forward echelon units with the 'Position' as parameter.
  • Rear echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "mobility support" mission to the engineer units, with the front units as parameters.
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the front units as parameter.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear line may receive the "combat support" mission (the enemy may be engaged),
    • In case of a front line unit destruction, combat units of the rear line may releive this unit.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all the subordinate agents have finished their mission.
