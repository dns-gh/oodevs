Mission : COMBAT - Block

Context

A mission assigned to a unit which requires it to deny enemy access to a given area or to prevent enemy advance in a given direction.
In Sword, to move and occupy an objective (or a set of objectives) and engage approaching enemies.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Objectives

Set of objectives to defend.

Meeting position

Position to reach by the command post and engineer units after performing their mission.

Positions

Positions from which the subordinate observe the given objectives.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Obstacles

Set of obstacles ot errect by engineer units (no obstacle by default).

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Block" mission to the armor platoons, with the objectives, positions and obstacles as parameters, (each of those parameters are shared between platoons).
    • The scouting platoons receive the "Monitor" mission with objectives and the main direction as parameters,
    • The engineer units receive the "Perform construction works" mission if obstacles have to be errected (otherwise they receive the "follow" mission) .
  • Rear echelon units:
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear echelon may receive the "combat support" mission(the enemy may be engaged).
    • In case of a first echelon unit destruction, combat units of the rear echelon may releive this unit.
  • All the agents share the same issued area of responsability.

Specific cases

Comments

The platoons will always spread across the positions (meaning that there will not be 2 platoons at the same position, except if there are less positions than forward echelon platoons) and pick the closest objectives to them.

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmantary order) is issued by user.
