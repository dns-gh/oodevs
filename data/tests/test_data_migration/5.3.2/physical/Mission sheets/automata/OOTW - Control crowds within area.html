Mission : OOTW - Control crowds within area

Context

To prevent the outbreak of disorder and prevention of possible riot.
In Sword, to wait for crowds to enter in a given area in order to keep them under control.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

The area into which the subordinate control the entering crowds.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Do not split area

Defines if the commander should not split the area into sub-areas. If it is set to 'TRUE' (default value is 'FALSE'), the agent issue the given area as the objective for subordinates missions.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Control crowds in area" mission to the subordinate units.
  • The area is splitted into sub-areas. Each of the subordinate unit receives a sub-area as mission objective.
  • The commander agent receive the "MoveTo" mission to reach the center of the area.
1) The agents occupy the subdivided area waiting for crowds to come :

2)Crowds start to enter the area:

3) Crowds exit the area, the agent returns in the center of their respective area :

Specific cases
  • If the "doNotSplitArea" is set to 'TRUE', the commander agent issues the entire 'Area' to its subordinates. In that case the subordinate units occupy the same area and may interact with encountered crowds together.

Comments

End of mission

The mission continues until another one is issued.
