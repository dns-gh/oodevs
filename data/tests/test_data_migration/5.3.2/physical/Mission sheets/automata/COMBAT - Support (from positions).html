Mission : COMBAT - Support (from positions)

Context

The action of a force which aids, protects, complements, or sustains another force in accordance with a directive requiring such action.
  • A unit which helps another unit in battle. Aviation, artillery, or naval gunfire may be used as a support for infantry.
  • A part of any unit held back at the beginning of an attack as a reserve.
  • An element of a command which assists, protects, or supplies other forces in combat.
In this mission, to provide DIRECT FIRE to a set of units, in order to destroy or neutralize approaching enemies. Each agent moves to a given position, and face the objective waiting for enemies to engage.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Friend company

Positions

Positions to be reached by forward echelon units.

Objective

Objectives to face.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Number of front line units

Define the number of foward echelon units (default value is "2").

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Support (from a position)" mission to the teeth arm platoons, with the objectives as parameters,
  • Rear echelon units:
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear echelon may receive the "combat support" mission(the enemy may be engaged).
    • In case of a first echelon unit destruction, combat units of the rear echelon may releive this unit.
    • All the agent share the same issued area of responsability.
