Mission : COMMON - Get decontaminated

Context

Units may be contaminated during an NBC incident. To avoid an NBC contamination and intoxication of other units (contamination can spread around and contaminate other units), contaminated units should be decontaminated with appropriate means.
In SWORD, an agent must go through a decontamination site object to get decontaminated. The decontamination process is effective if the decontamination site is operated by other units.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Decontamination site

Decontamination site to use to get decontaminated.

Meeting point

Position to reach after being decontaminated.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "get decontaminated" mission to all its surbordinates.
  • If subordinate units can't perform the mission, they receive the "follow" mission with the other units as parameter.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all first echelon units have reached their objective.
