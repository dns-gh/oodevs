Mission : COMBAT - Harass enemies in area

Context

Mission to destroy or at least to neutralize the enemy hidden elements, infiltrated or during infiltration and exfiltration in a given area.
In SWORD, to observe an area and to occupy positions in order to engage identified units in this area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

The area inside which the subordinates harass encountered enemies.

Meeting position

The position to be reached by rear echelon units (such as the command post unit)

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Positions

The positions from which the subordinate units harass encountered enemies.

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units:
        • First case: positions are issued.
          • The commander agent issues the "Harass enemies in area" mission to the armor platoons, with the area and positions as parameters. Positions are fairly shared between platoons.
        • Second case: no position issued.
          • The commander agent computes sub-area into the provided area and issues the "Harass enemies in area" mission to the armor platoons, with a sub-area as parameters (one sub area per subordinate unit).
  • Rear echelon units:
  • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "moveTo" mission with the with the meeting position as parameter.
      • Units able to perform indirect fires (such as mortar troops) receive the "Provide indirect fires (dynamic)" mission with harassing units as parameter.
  • All the agents share the same issued area of responsability.

Comments
  • If no position issued, the subordinate units tries to retreive key positions inside the area. Key positions are defined by:
    • crossroads
    • roads intersections with the company area
    • urban blocks.
Beware before issuing this mission to choose carefully the area. If area is located in open terrain without many roads inside, the mission won't be performed in a good way if user don't designate the positions.

End of mission

The mission terminates when all subordinate units have reached the meeting position.
