Mission : SAFETY - Decontaminate

Context

To depollute or decontaminate in order to rehabilitate a site or a soil that has been polluted by industrial activities. The decontamination of a site reduces the risk of pollution to acceptable levels or make it disappear.
In SWORD, decontaminating a site ("contaminated site") prevents units from being contaminated. It prevents the creation and the dispersion of a contaminated cloud that can cause injuries in units. A "decontaminated site" in SWORD is defined by a surface delimited by a polygon. All contamination objects (such as "contaminated area") that intersect with the decontaminated surface lose their contamination capacity.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Sites to decontaminate

Areas (polygons) or urban blocks to decontaminate.

Rally point

Position to reach after performing the mission.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues a "Decontaminate" order to each capable agent. There are two cases :
    • If there are strictly more sites to decontaminate than capable units, all the units will be provided one site to decontaminate. As soon as a unit has finished to decontaminate an area, another site will be assigned to him, unless there is no more sites left to decontaminate, in which case he will receive a *Move to* order to the rally point.
    • Otherwise, the sites will all be provided to the units at the beginning of the mission. They will be fairly distributed among them, and if they are any area (polygon) to decontaminate, it may be split so that multiple units can work on them. Only one unit can work on a urban block at the same time, therefore if there are only urban blocks to decontaminate and no areas, there may be units with no sites to decontaminate, in which case the leftover units will receive a *Move To* order to the rally point instead.
  • The other units are given a "Move To" order to the rally point.

End of mission
