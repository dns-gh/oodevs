Mission : TRAFIC - Support movement in areas

Context

All actions performed by the platoons in trafic control to block off areas for friend agents.
In SWORD, to create movement enhanced areas where friend agents move inside it at their maximum speed.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

the right and left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Movement enhanced areas

Set of movement enhanced area objects to perform.

Optional

Phase lines

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "support movement in area" mission to the subordinate platoons, with the given movement enhanced area as parameter.
  • Other platoons are ordered to reinforce or follow.

Specific cases
  • If more movement enhanced areas are given than subordinate platoons, some of movement enhanced areas will not given as parameter to subordinate platoons.
  • If less movement enhanced areas are given than subordinate platoons, some subordinates platoons which can perform the "support movement in area" mission will received reinforce or follow.

Comments
  • Each platoon is given only one movement enhanced area.
  • The movement enhanced area is randomly chosen by the commander agent).

End of mission

The mission continues until another one is issued.
