Mission : COMMON - Collect information

Context

To gather important information about a specific situation, units may interrogate prisoners or collect information from civilians and local authorities.
In SWORD, to get a specific message information from a unit or a crowd. An interrogated unit or crowd display a message writen by trainers, in order to provide indication(s) to trainees about the situation. The interrogated unit can represent a local authority, a journalist etc.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Entity (crowd or unit)

Unit or crowd to get the information from.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Collect information" mission to one of its surbordinates. Other units receive the "follow" mission with the other units as parameter.

Specific cases

Comments
  • Gather information from a unit takes time. This delay depends on:
    • the affinity of the interrogated unit regarding to the agent's camp: the more the interrogated unit has affinity with the agent, the shortest it is to get the information (see delays in authoring tool).
    • the diplomacy matrix: an enemy cannot be interrogated if the force ratio of the agent (the interrogator) is low.

End of mission

The mission ends when the interrogator unit (the one that has received the mission) get the wanted information.
