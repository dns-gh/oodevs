Mission : AAA - Defend area

Context

To defend Army ground forces against attack by enemy aircraft.
In Sword, to move to and occupy an observation position in order to detect and engage flying enemies within the given area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

The area to defend against enemies aircrafts.

Meeting position

The position to be reached by the CP.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Positions

The positions from which the surbordinate units observe the area.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Defend area" mission to the subordinate platoons, with a position as parameter. Each platoon is given a position. If no position issued, the commander spilts the area into subareas. Each sub-area is given to a subbordinate unit as an observing position.
  • The command post unit moves to the meeting position.
  • Other units are ordered to follow.

Specific cases

Here some examples of possible deployments. The behaviors is the same for the "AAA- Monitor area" (used screenshots are taken from this mission).
  • Use cases 1 : no positions issued, open terrain. The commander agent splits the area into sub-areas. Each subordinate units occupies a sub-area facing the main area barycenter.

  • Use cases 2 : urban blocks have been issued as positions to occupy. Each subordinate units occupies a urban block, facing the area to monitor.

  • Use cases 3 : urban blocks have been issued as positions to occupy. Each subordinate units occupies a urban block, facing the area to monitor.

Comments

End of mission

The mission continues until another one is issued.
