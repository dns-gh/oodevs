Mission : ENGINEER - Open a route

Context

To reduce or negate the effects of existing or reinforcing obstacles. The objectives are to maintain freedom of movement for maneuver units, weapons systems and critical supplies.
In Sword, to move along a computed path and bypass obstacles that collide with the path. Here, 'obstacles' are sword 'objects' that have the capacity "avoidable" and/or "constuctor".

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Waypoints

Set of waypoints defining the route to follow (the path will be computed taking into account those waypoints)

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Open a route" mission to the engineer platoons, with a set of waypoints as parameter.
  • Because few subordinate agents can perform the same works, opening a route with a engineer company can be very efficient (in term of delays).
  • When the last waypoints have been reached, the mission terminates.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission terminates when all subordinates has finished their mission.
