Mission : AAA - Monitor area

Context

The systematic observation of aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic, or other means.
To provide continuous view, and the potential for reports on the activity of an objective.
In Sword, to move to and occupy an observation position in order to detect flying enemies within the given area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

Set of positions to use to self deploy.

Meeting position

Position to be reached by the CP.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Positions

Firing position to be reached by subordinate units.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Monitor area" mission to the subordinate platoons, with a position as parameter. Each platoon is given a position. If no position issued, the commander spilts the area into subareas. Each sub-area is given to a subbordinate unit as an observing position.
  • The command post unit moves to the meeting position.
  • Other units are ordered to follow.

Specific cases

Here some example of possible deployments.
  • Use cases 1 : no positions issued, open terrain. The commander agent splits the area into sub-areas. Each subordinate units occupies a sub-area facing the main area barycenter.

  • Use cases 2 : urban blocks have been issued as positions to occupy. Each subordinate units occupies a urban block, facing the area to monitor.

  • Use cases 3 : urban blocks have been issued as positions to occupy. Each subordinate units occupies a urban block, facing the area to monitor.

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends if another mission (or "the cancel maneuver" fragmantary order) is issued by user.
