Mission : COMBAT - Support Company (dynamic)

Context

The action of a force which aids, protects, complements, or sustains another force in accordance with a directive requiring such action.
  • A unit which helps another unit in battle. Aviation, artillery, or naval gunfire may be used as a support for infantry.
  • A part of any unit held back at the beginning of an attack as a reserve.
  • An element of a command which assists, protects, or supplies other forces in combat.
In SWORD, supporting a friend is to provide intelligence and direct fires, in order to destroy or neutralize dangerous enemies, by moving from one supporting position to another.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Company

Friend company to support.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • Forward echelon subordinates units:
    • the commander agent issues the "Support (dynamic)" mission to its forward echelon units with the forward echelon units of the supported company as paramters.
  • Rear echelon subordinates units:
    • the commander agent issues the "mobility support" mission to the engineer units, with the forward echelon units as parameters.
    • other units (reserve and non adapted units receive the "follow" mission with the forward echelon units as parameter.
    • in case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the second echelon may receive the "combat support" mission (the enemy may be engaged)
  • Upon issuing the mission, the commander agent coordinates its subordinate units.

End of mission
