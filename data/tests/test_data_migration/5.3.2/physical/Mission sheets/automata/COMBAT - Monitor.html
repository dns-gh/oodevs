Mission : COMBAT - Monitor

Context

The systematic observation of aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic, or other means.
To provide continuous view, and the potential for reports on the activity of an objective.
In this mission, to observe an objective (a point, urban block, an area etc.) with visual means. The agents simply orientate their sensors toward the objective from adaptated positions.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Objective

Objective to observe.

Position(s)

Set of positions from which to observe the objective.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units:
    • The commander agent issues the "Monitor" mission to armored and scout platoons, with the objectives as parameters,
  • Rear echelon units:
    • Other units (reserve and non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter.
    • In case of an attack by an enemy, combat units of the rear echelon may receive the "combat support" mission(the enemy may be engaged).
    • In case of a first echelon unit destruction, combat units of the rear echelon may releive this unit.
  • All the agent share the same issued area of responsability.

End of mission
