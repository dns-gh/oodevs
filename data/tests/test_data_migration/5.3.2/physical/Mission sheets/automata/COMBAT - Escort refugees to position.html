Mission : COMBAT - Escort refugees to position

Context

A member of the Armed Forces assigned to accompany, assist, or guide an individual or group.
An armed guard that accompanies a convoy, a train, prisoners, etc.
In SWORD, to accompany a refugees or surrendered units toward a specific location (a position or a camp for instance).

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Location

Location into which the agent has to accompany the refugees units.

Refugees

Refugees units to be transported to the given location.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • Forward echelon units
    • The commander agent issues the "EscortRefugeesToPosition" mission to one unit, with all refugees as parameters (only one first echelon unit in this mission).
  • Rear echelon units
    • the commander agent issues the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter.
    • in case of an attack by an enemy or bad force ratio, combat units of the second echelon may receive the "combat support" mission (the enemy may be engaged in return).

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all refugees units have been unloaded into the camp.
