Mission : HUMINT - Collect informations (in area)

Context

The systematic observation of aerospace, surface or subsurface areas, places, persons, or things, by visual, aural, electronic, photographic, or other means.
To provide continuous view, and the potential for reports on the activity of an objective.
In this Sword mission, to move to an area and get information about the presence of units within it. It is used to simulate the capability of human intelligence units to gather informations, by applying a magic effect in the area. This effects allows the subordinate units to detect (quickly) others units in the area.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

Area to get the informations from.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Infiltration mode

Defines if the agent uses infiltration to reach the barycenter of the area (defaut value is FALSE).

Behavior
  • Foward echelon units: the commander agent issues the "Collect informations (in area)" mission to all subordinate units able to perform the mission., with the objectives as parameters.
  • Rear echelon units: other units (non adapted units) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as parameter.
  • Command post: in this mission, the command post unit stays on its own postion.
  • All the agent share the same issued area of responsability.
