Mission : AAVN - Attack

Context

An offensive action characterized by movement supported by fire.
In SWORD, to move towards a provided area, engage all enemies inside and go back to a rally point.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Area

The area inside of which the perceived enemies will be engaged.

Release point

The position where the subordinates will stop their movement in column formation and go their separate ways.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Rally point

The rally point to go to once the furthest border of the area has been reached. If this parameter is not provided, then the subordinate will go to its initial position instead.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Move in column formation" mission with the release point as the only waypoint.
  • Once the release point is reached by all units, the commander agent issues the "Attack" mission to each subordinate, each with a subdivision of the provided area. Each subordinate will move to the center of their sub-area, then to the furthest border of the area (in order to reveal the maximum number of enemies), and finally back to the rally point or to the initial position if no rally point was provided.

The subordinates move in a column formation to the release point.

The subordinates go to their sub-area and then move to the rally point.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends once all the units have ended their "Attack" mission.
