Mission : ASY - Infiltrate

Context

A process in which individuals (or small groups) penetrate an area (especially the military penetration of enemy positions without detection)
In Sword, to move to an objective using a "infiltration" type of movement.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Waypoints

Path to move along.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues a "Infiltrate" order to each subordinate with the route as parameter. Non adapted units (in case some subordinate are not able to perform the mission) receive the "follow" mission with the first echelon units as a parameter (units that are able to perform the mission.

Specific cases

Comments
  • If one of the subordinate goes through a checkpoint, it may be detected and captured.

End of mission

The mission ends when all the subordinate units have finished their mission.
