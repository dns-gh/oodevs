Mission : COMMON - Transport (a crowd)

Context

To provide general transport service in the movement of personnel and cargo.
In SWORD, transporting crowds allows to move them to a given location with appropriate means. Because the agent can only transports crowds' concentrations, this mission needs a loading position to indicate which concentration to transport. Indeed, a crowd can be compouded with several separated concentrations and only one concentration can be transported at a time. The agent will transport the nearest concentration from the "loadingPoint" parameter.

Parameters

Mandatory

Danger direction

Expected enemy direction.

Limit 1

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Limit 2

Right or left boundaries inside which the agent perform the mission.

Crowd

Crowd to transport.

Loading position

Position to reach to load the troops.

Route

Destination into which the agent have to transport the units.

Meeting position

Position to reach after the troops transportation.

Optional

Phase lines

Lines used by units to adapt their behavior when they reach or cross them.

Behavior
  • The commander agent issues the "Transport" mission to all subordinate units able to perform it. The more units perform the mission, the fastest the transportation is.
  • Subordinate units that cannot perform the transportation mission move directly to the meeting position.

Specific cases

Comments

End of mission

The mission ends when all units have reached the meeting position.
