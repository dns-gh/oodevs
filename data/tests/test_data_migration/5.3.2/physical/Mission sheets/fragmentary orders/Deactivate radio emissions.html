Mission : Deactivate radio emissions

Context

For the agent, to switch the radio communication off.

Parameters

Mandatory

Optional

Behavior
  • Once the agent has switch off the radio, it cannot transmit information to teammates agents.
  • It means that collected informations such as enemies knowledge won't be shared with teammate units of the agent's knowledge group (actually, the agent with a "radio silence" status has its own knowledge group).
  • The speed of movement is slowed down too.
