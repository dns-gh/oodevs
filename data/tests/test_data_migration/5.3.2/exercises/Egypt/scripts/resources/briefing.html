Sword OT demo : Egypt

Introduction

This scenario takes place in North Egypt, with an opposition between a blue force, a local red asymetric force and a neutral population.

The following scores are calculated:

  • Opstate Red
  • Opstate Combat Blue
  • Opstate All Blue
  • Mean Blue MBT Gasoil Resources
  • Distance Blue CT - Blue MBT
  • Blue Maintenance
  • Blue MBT Total Damages
  • Blue Direct Fire Unit
